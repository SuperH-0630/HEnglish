What is this?

HEnglish is an English learning engine, and this website is an implementation of that engine.

How it helps us learn English?

It helps us memorize words

How to ues it?

First register an account, select the word bank (template), and then log in to the account. At this point you will see the words displayed in English. Try to recall the Chinese meaning of the word in your mind, then click answer to see if you remember the word correctly (note that you only have 120s), and select right, wrong (if you want to skip a word, you can click Next word, if you want to delete it Words can click Delete word, if you want to download words, you can click Download). The system will divide the words into 5 levels according to your familiarity. Words of different levels will appear at different frequencies. Click info to view some English example sentences of the word, which is helpful for memory. Click on user to see the user's information, as well as the glossary. You can click csv, xlsx, etc. to download word documents in different formats. You can reset the user and re-memorize the words.
